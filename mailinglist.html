Posting a Message

Follow these steps:
  1. Familiarize yourself with the Netiquette Guidelines before posting. Even seasoned Internet users should reread this document from time to time.
  2. Subscribe to the mailing list.
  3. Post your message by sending email to opensource@franz.com.

List Charter

This mailing list is for discussing the software available through http://opensource.franz.com.  Bugs reports for Allegro CL should be sent to bugs@franz.com and not this mailing list.

Other List Policies

No flaming!
Flamers may be blocked or unsubscribed.
Returned mail
If list mail is returned, you may be unsubscribed without warning. Permanent SMTP (500 series) errors will result in immediate removal. Temporary SMTP (400 series) errors will result in removal if condition is not resolved within a reasonable amount of time (a couple of days).
Vacation responses
If you send a vacation or other automated response in reply to a list message, you may be unsubscribed without warning.
No spam!
Do not post commercial advertisements without approval of list owner. Spammers may be blocked or unsubscribed.
Privacy
All postings are considered public statements of their authors.

We use subscriber information only to validate and distribute list messages. We do not share subscriber information, including e-mail addresses.
